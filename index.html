Who we are

We are a dedicated organization committed to saving lives through the selfless act of blood donation. Our organization collects blood for hospitals in Prague, and we need your support. We believe that every person has the ability to make a positive impact. Join us in this important cause, and let's work together to make a meaningful impact on the health and well-being of those who depend on donated blood.
